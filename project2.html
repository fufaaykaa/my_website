Overview

This game was created by Taya Malakhova, Marianna Koszutska, and Marta Vedral in PuzzleScript. It is a 2D puzzle game that involves solving maze-like levels. The player is a zombie virus that infects a healthy human's body by leaving a green trail behind it. There are 9 levels in total, each being a different body part. Can you solve all of them? Let's find out!

Unique
Gameplay
Mechanic

  • Unique way of interaction with each level asset
  • The virus leaves a green trail behind, representing the infection spreading
  • Multiple ways of completing each level

Puzzle-Based
Level Design

  • Maze-like puzzles that challenge players to infect all required areas
  • 10 unique levels, each representing a different body part
  • Increasing difficulty with more complex layouts in later levels
  • Each move matters, forcing strategic decisions

Distinct
Art Style

  • Minimalistic pixel art
  • Green infection trail contrasts with the human body’s background
  • Every asset in the level follows the logic of the narrative
