Overview

What Dark Light hopes to be is a game where you solve puzzles to uncover the story of a post-apocalyptic world where the sun disappeared.

Puzzle-
Solving
Mechanics

  • Multiple puzzles in the game
  • Each puzzle is unique and has its own mechanic and purpose
  • Puzzles are integrated in the narrative
  • Puzzles influence the environment and progression through the game

Exploration &
Environmental
Storytelling

  • The narrative idea is explained to the players through locations and objects
  • The more the player explores, the more detailed and clear the story becomes

Atmospheric
World Design

  • The main locations translate the atmosphere of the post-apocalyptic world
  • Uses hand-crafted 3D models, minimalistic textures, and a small amount of light

Interactive &
Animated
Environment

  • Objects and locations react to player's interactions
  • Examples: Electricity box, fences, barn doors, street lights

Dialogue
System

  • Guides the player through the mystery and adds details to the story
  • Focuses the player's attention on the main questions

Immersive
Sound &
Visual Effects

  • Lighting and shading simulate a dark, eerie atmosphere
  • Sound design enhances immersion with ambient and environmental sounds

3D Modeling

  • Farm Location: Barn, greenhouses, tractor, 3 gardens, 4 types of bushes, 3 types of trees, 3 types of fences, 2 types of boxes, street lights, gnome, ladder, and all other models in the "Farm" location.
  • Water Elements: Created water model and effects.
  • Magic Fountain Location: Designed the fountain, flowers, and trees with flowers.
  • Forest Location: Fishing rod, puzzle models for the cave, 2 types of fences, and reused some trees and bushes from the farm.

Animations

  • Character waking up in the beginning.
  • Switcher turning and lighting in the greenhouse.
  • Opening and closing of the electricity box.
  • Fence fixing in the garden.
  • Gnome's hat movement.
  • Barn doors opening animation.
  • Street lights and tractor lights switching on.
  • Water movement in the fountain.
  • Sun rising back into the sky.
  • Particle systems.
  • Additional animations throughout the game.

Texturing

  • Created textures and UV mapping for all 3D models.
  • Designed ground textures for different locations.

Coding

  • Implemented the dialogue system.
  • Programmed triggers for animations.
  • Developed doors opening and closing mechanics.
  • Created text prompts for controls that appear and disappear.

User Interface (UI)

  • Designed the interactive panel with a code entry system.
  • Designed dialogues UI.
  • Created and managed the final cutscene.

Sound Design

  • Added sounds for the animations.
  • Set up audio manager.
  • 3D audio.
